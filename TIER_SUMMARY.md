## How Features Are Protected

All features are automatically checked based on your subscription tier:

1. **API Routes**: Check your tier before allowing actions
2. **UI Components**: Hide/disable features you don't have access to
3. **Usage Limits**: Track and enforce contract/template limits
4. **Error Messages**: Guide you to upgrade if needed

### Example:
- Free tier users trying to use AI will see: "AI contract generation is only available for paid subscribers"
- Starter tier users hitting 20 contract limit will see: "Limit exceeded: 20/20 contracts. Upgrade to Pro to continue."

---

## Trial Information

- **Starter, Pro, and Premium** all include a **7-day free trial**
- Trial gives you full access to that tier's features
- Trial can only be used once per account
- After trial ends, you'll be charged unless you cancel
